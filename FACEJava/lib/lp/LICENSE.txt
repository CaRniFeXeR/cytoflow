The QSopt authors have granted FACEJava permission to use and redistribute 
QSopt as part of FACEJava. Developers using FACEJava as a library are also
allowed to include QSopt in their distributions but are not allowed to use the
QSopt library in any other way. That is, they may not use QSopt directly. If
they wish to make use of QSopt for other LP problems, they must accept the
QSopt license. Ordinarily, QSopt is free for reasearch or education purposes.
